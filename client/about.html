About Dr. Debate

Dr. Debate is an AI‑powered debate assistant that helps you practice and refine your argumentative skills in a solo environment.

Enter any topic you want to debate and our system will:

  • Generate targeted research queries and fetch evidence from the web
  • Engage you with AI opponents taking pro and con positions
  • Allow you to highlight key points for on‑the‑fly analysis
  • Provide real‑time feedback and post‑debate evaluations
  • Offer a library of historical debates enriched with AI-driven insights and an interactive agent you can query for summaries, key takeaways, and rhetorical analysis

Our unique AI integration extends beyond simple playback: you can ask our intelligent assistant to summarize specific arguments, analyze debate strategies, or highlight pivotal moments in any past debate, all in real time.

After each session, the system generates a detailed performance report, pinpointing your strengths, areas for improvement, and providing tailored exercises to elevate your debating prowess.

Whether you're preparing for a competition, honing your public speaking, or simply sharpening your critical thinking, Dr. Debate adapts to your pace and guides you toward mastering the art of persuasion with personalized insights and interactive support.
