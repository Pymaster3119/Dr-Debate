What is Dr. Debate?

Dr. Debate is an AI‑powered debate assistant designed to help you fine-tune your argumentative skills. Whether you’re preparing for a debate competition or seeking to sharpen your critical thinking, our platform delivers dynamic sessions that challenge and inspire.

How It Works

  • Generate targeted research queries and fetch evidence from the web.
  • Engage with AI opponents taking both pro and con stances.
  • Highlight key points with on‑the‑fly analysis.
  • Receive real‑time feedback and comprehensive post‑debate evaluations.
  • Explore a rich library of historical debates enriched with AI insights.

Our Vision

We believe that effective communication and critical thinking are the keys to success. By merging cutting‑edge AI with the art of debate, we empower every individual to become a confident speaker and a skilled thinker.
